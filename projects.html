Twitch Better Notify

Desktop app to monitor Twitch channels in real time

Twitch Better Notify was built to improve your experience when following Twitch live streams. It runs quietly in the background and gives you fast, reliable alerts when your favorite channels go live.

  • Instant notifications when a channel goes live
  • Online/offline status list with clear color indicators
  • Optional auto-open stream when you click the notification
  • Runs minimized to the system tray
  • Simple channel management: add, remove, monitor
